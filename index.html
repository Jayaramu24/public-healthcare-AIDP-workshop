Business use case

Prioritize care access, claims, disbursement, membership, provider oversight, staffing, outreach, and quality.

MPHA leadership needs a shared view of facility pressure, immunization equity, respiratory surveillance, claims, public-program payments, membership eligibility, provider accreditation, and quality events so teams can act before financial, access, or service risk escalates.

  • Identify facilities exceeding wait-time or occupancy targets.
  • Compare public-health pressure across districts and reporting weeks.
  • Track immunization campaign reach by district and age group.
  • Connect respiratory positivity trends to emergency demand.
  • Surface service-quality events that need faster closure.
  • Spot claims denial patterns and disbursement exceptions by coverage program.
  • Monitor membership renewal risk and high-risk member segments.
  • Review provider accreditation scores, corrective actions, and expiry risk.
Architecture

Medallion architecture from raw data to business insights.

Raw files land unchanged, AI Data Platform creates Bronze and Silver Delta layers, and the Gold business layer is set up in AI Lakehouse as a dimensional star schema for OAC dashboards and generative summaries.

Bronze data and logic

Created in AIDP from the raw object-storage landing zone.

  • Tables: CSV operations, claims, disbursement, membership, provider accreditation, JSON capacity events, GeoJSON spatial features, and playbook chunks.
  • Logic: preserve raw values, nested payloads, geometry, source file, ingest batch, timestamp, and reject only unreadable rows.

Silver data and logic

Created in AIDP as conformed, validated Delta tables.

  • Tables: facility, daily operations, immunization, district health, quality, claims, disbursement, membership, accreditation, capacity event, spatial, and playbook tables.
  • Logic: parse dates and JSON, extract GeoJSON features, validate rates and amounts, join program and member segments, deduplicate keys, and calculate operational flags.

Gold data and logic

Created in AI Lakehouse as shared dimensions, fact tables, bridge tables, and OAC-ready star views.

  • Tables: shared dimensions, coverage program, claim type, member segment, accreditation status, business facts, and chat bridge tables.
  • Logic: reuse conformed dimensions, publish payer/provider fact tables, expose star views, and preserve flat marts only for quick validation.
Data quality checks protect dates, rates, occupancy values, and row-count expectations.
AIDP owns Bronze and Silver transformation logic; AI Lakehouse owns the Gold star schema and OAC star views.
Synthetic claim and membership identifiers keep the workshop free of names, addresses, medical-record numbers, and PHI.

Workshop outline

Core labs plus optional real-time analytics extensions.

Lab 1 · 45 minutes

Create the healthcare lakehouse

Prepare the environment, upload raw MPHA operations and payer/provider datasets, and profile the source files.

  • Create an object storage folder.
  • Upload five CSV files, one JSONL file, one GeoJSON file, and one DOCX file.
  • Confirm claims, disbursement, membership, and accreditation row counts.
  • Read and validate row counts.
Lab 2 · 75 minutes

Build Bronze and Silver in AIDP

Use Spark and Delta Lake to ingest raw data, conform references, validate fields, and derive reusable measures.

  • Create Bronze Delta tables.
  • Create Silver conformed tables.
  • Stage dimensional Gold facts, dimensions, spatial, event, and document-chat outputs.
Lab 3 · 60 minutes

Set up dimensional Gold and gather insights

Create the AI Lakehouse star schema, build OAC canvases, and generate leadership narratives.

  • Load Gold dimensions and facts.
  • Create interactive OAC tabs and filters.
  • Summarize actions from metrics and playbook chunks.
Optional Lab 4 · 45-60 minutes

Real-time analytics with GoldenGate

Capture source operational changes and publish real-time Gold serving objects.

  • Configure source change capture.
  • Deliver CDC to stream, storage, or AI Lakehouse staging.
  • Feed the real-time OAC dashboard tab.
Optional Lab 5 · 45-60 minutes

Stream analytics with Kafka via AIDP

Process wait-time telemetry through a streaming medallion flow.

  • Publish Kafka-compatible wait-time events.
  • Create streaming Bronze, Silver, and Gold layers.
  • Monitor live pressure bands in OAC.
